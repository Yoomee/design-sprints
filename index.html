Rapidly validate your ideas

Working with a team of digital experts test your ideas and assumptions using the Design Sprint™ process tailored to fit your non-profit organisation

Day 1

Discover

Identify the problems you are facing and set yourself up to create a solution

Day 2

Explore

As a group bring to light possible solutions to solve your problem

Day 3

Distill

Pull together the best parts of your ideas until you have one testable answer

Day 4

Prototype

Create what's required to allow users to test your solution

Day 5

Test

Put your prototype in front of real users and see how they respond
