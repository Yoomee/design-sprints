Validate your ideas. Quickly.

Think you've got a great idea for your charity or nonprofit? Go through our week-long Design Sprint process and let's find out together.

Day 1

Discover

Identify the problems and learn everything you need to know to create a solution.

Day 2

Explore

Work as a group to come up with possible solutions to your problem.

Day 3

Distill

Bring the best parts of your ideas together until you have one testable answer.

Day 4

Prototype

Create a working prototype of your solution that allows real users to get hands-on and test your idea.

Day 5

Test

Put your prototype in front of real users, ask important questions and see how they respond.
